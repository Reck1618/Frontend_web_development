Prophet

Major Laurence "Prophet" Barnes is the squad leader of Raptor Team during the events of Crysis on the Lingshan Islands and a major character in both Crysis and Crysis 2. A copy of his personality and memories returns as the protagonist in Crysis 3 within the Nanosuit 2.0.

Being a good soldier comes down to one thing.
To one single question: What are you prepared to sacrifice?
When they came to me with the nanosuit, I sacrificed Laurence Barnes, the man I was, to become Prophet.
When my own flesh and blood held me back, I sacrificed that too.
Replaced it, like a spare part.
Victory costs.
Everytime, you pay a little more.
I saw a glimpse of what's coming and there was nothing left of me to stop it.

what do we do then?
